Guidelines

Only first 15 schools to register will be allowed to participate in the event.

A maximum of 2.5 minutes will be provided to each speaker. Marks will be deducted for exceeding the time limit.

There will be maximum of 2 interjections per speaker. One cross question will be allowed per speaker which will not carry any marks for the interjector but will affect the rebuttal of the speaker.

In the absence of an interjection, the speaker will be awarded full marks in the rebuttal criterion. Speakers are not allowed to read from a script or directly refer to the presentation. Non-adherence to this rule will lead to deduction of marks.

Registration will be given on first-come-first-serve basis.

All presentations to be prepared in MS PowerPoint 2007 or lower and must be carried in a pen drive only.

Participants are to choose only one topic from
Hail to the economic revolution "One nation, One Tax" GST.
Corruption : The dark knight vs nemesis technology
Art and music are presentation of the human emotion not machine automation.
Game Theory : The business implication that a move by one player strikes off moves by others.

Duration: 2.5 minutes
Awards will be given for: Best Speaker, Second Best Speaker, Best Interjector and Best Visual Aid Supporter
Judging Criteria: Innovation, Content, Delivery and Rebuttal
