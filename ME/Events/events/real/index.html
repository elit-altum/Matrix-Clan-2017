Real The UnReal

“It’s Nothing is so unbelievable that oratory cannot make it acceptable”.

No. Of Teams

1

Participants Per Team

2

Eligibility

Open

Guidelines

Prelims –
The goal is to produce a “research” paperrelated to the idea that has been decided. A PowerPoint presentation or a video can also be attached. The word limit is 15k words.

The paper along with any attached files has to be sent via e-mail to thematrixclan@gmail.com

The top 6 teams will be selected for the finals.

Finals-
A talk has to be presented with a PowerPoint presentation and/or a video. Props may also be used.

The team can have a maximum of 2 members (including any prop setter or video/ppt controller).

The main topic cannot be real/true. It should strictly beUnReal. Actual facts have to be moulded in a way that they persuade the judges in believing the unreal statement.

Duration: The talk has an upper limit of 6 minutes.

Judging Criteria: Far Sightedness, Twisting Ability And Moulding Of Topic, Targeted Market
