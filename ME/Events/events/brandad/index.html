Guidelines

The participants must prepare and perform an advertisement in English to attract the allotted target audience. Points will be deducted for usage of Hindi.

Exceeding time limit will lead to deduction of points.

Teams must make use of visuals. It can be either done through a self-made video (of max 30 seconds) or a poster of relevance to attract the target audience, which must be made use of within the allotted time frame.

Teams may use costumes, props, charts, musical instruments, jingles or taglines.

There will be a surprise twist given to the teams at the time of registration..

Allotments will be given on a first come first served basis. After registration, the participants should contact the concerned in-charge mentioned below to receive their topic.

Judging Criteria: Creativity, Content, Presentation, Use of tagline/jingle, Use of Visual-Aid, Use of surprise factor.
